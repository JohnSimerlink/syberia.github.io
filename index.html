Build predictive models faster than ever before.

Syberia is a framework for quickly trying different methods in data science.

Syberia supports building, debugging, testing, and deploying classifiers developed in R. It includes support for importing data, cleaning/transforming data, fitting models to data, validating the results, and deployment as a REST service.

Syberia makes it easy to quickly clean up messy data, build produciton-ready predictive models, and quickly skip past the boring stuff and onto the interesting data analysis.
